FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

How far in advance should I book?

+

We recommend booking 3-6 months in advance for weddings and large events, especially during peak season (May-October). However, we often have availability for last-minute bookings. Contact us to check our calendar!

What's included in your photo booth packages?

+

Our packages typically include: unlimited photos during rental period, professional attendant, custom photo templates, digital copies of all photos, props (if requested), and instant printing or digital delivery. Specific inclusions vary by package.

Do you travel outside of Denver?

+

Yes! We serve all of Colorado and travel nationwide for events. Travel fees may apply for locations outside our standard service area. We've done events from coast to coast!

How much space do you need?

+

Space requirements vary by equipment. Most photo booths need about 8x8 feet, 360 video booths need 10x10 feet, and larger installations like AI photo booths may need 10x12 feet. We'll work with your venue to find the perfect spot!

Can you customize the photo templates?

+

Absolutely! All our packages include custom photo templates featuring your event details, logos, colors, or branding. We work with you to create the perfect design that matches your event theme.

Technical Questions

What power requirements do you have?

+

Most of our equipment requires a standard 110V outlet. For outdoor events or venues without power, we can provide battery packs or generators. Let us know your venue details and we'll ensure we have the right setup!

Do you provide an attendant?

+

Yes! All our rentals include a professional attendant who will set up, operate, and manage the equipment throughout your event. They'll ensure everything runs smoothly and assist your guests.

How do guests receive their photos?

+

Guests can receive photos via instant print, text message, email, QR code download, or social media sharing - depending on which option you choose. Many clients opt for multiple delivery methods to maximize guest engagement.

Pricing & Payment

How much does a photo booth cost?

+

Pricing varies based on equipment type, rental duration, customization level, and location. Most packages start around $600-$800 for 2-3 hours. Premium options like 360 video or AI photo booths range from $1,200-$2,500. Contact us for a custom quote!

What's your cancellation policy?

+

We understand things happen! Cancellations made 60+ days before your event receive a full refund. Cancellations 30-60 days out receive 50% refund. Cancellations within 30 days are non-refundable. Rescheduling is always free!
