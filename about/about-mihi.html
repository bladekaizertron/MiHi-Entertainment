Our Story

Founded in Denver, Colorado, MiHi Entertainment has grown from a small photo booth rental company to one of the region's most trusted names in event entertainment. Our journey began with a simple mission: to help people create unforgettable memories at their most important celebrations.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of events annually across Colorado and nationwide, from intimate weddings in mountain resorts to massive corporate activations at trade shows. Our commitment to innovation, quality, and exceptional service has made us the go-to choice for event planners, venues, and hosts.
